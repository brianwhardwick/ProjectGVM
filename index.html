GVM Calculator for 4WD Vehicles

Choosing accessories for your 4WD? This site helps you stay safe, legal, and within weight limits.

Free GVM Calculator

Use the free GVM calculator to see how each modification affects your total vehicle weight and axle loading in real time.

Built by an enthusiast, for the 4WD community

"I built this tool while struggling to balance the weight on my own 200 Series LandCruiser build. As an engineer, I realized that sticking to GVM limits shouldn't require complex spreadsheets or guesswork. I wanted to create something visual, accurate, and free to help other 4WDers plan their dream rigs safely. Whether you are fitting out a new ute or checking your current setup, this tool is designed to help you hit the tracks with confidence."
